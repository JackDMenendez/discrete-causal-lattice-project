## Decisions & flags
- **`backward-midpoint-structurally-confirmed-full-close-at-exp03` (FLAG —
  UPDATE to the Phase-1 sign flag).** Test #4 (structural) confirms the Peierls
  coupling produces the **correct Q-tensor eigenstructure**: induced
  susceptibility is **uniaxial about (1,1,-1)** (perp1/perp2 = 0.997) and
  **axial-large / prolate** (axis/perp = 1.62) — matching Paper I's {4,4,16}
  where the large (16) eigenvalue is axial. This is strong evidence the
  **backward midpoint `½(A(x)+A(x−v))` is the right convention** (a wrong one
  would break uniaxiality or flip prolate->oblate). The Phase-1 flag is thus
  **downgraded from "unverified" to "structurally confirmed"**. It is **fully
  closed only by exp_03** (the exact {4,4,16} ratio + rigorous sign needs the
  vacuum-averaged induced action). Prime suspect remains the midpoint direction
  if exp_03's Q-tensor comes out wrong.
- **`q-tensor-exact-ratio-deferred-to-exp03` (FLAG).** Test #4 pins the
  *structure* (axis + uniaxial + prolate sign), NOT the exact `16/4 = 4` ratio.
  Reason: a single-wavepacket probe with an L2-norm functional is not the
  vacuum average, and the ratio needs Paper I's `induced_gauge_action.py`
  normalisation. The exact-eigenvalue cross-check is exp_03 work. Test #4's
  docstring states this scope explicitly.
- **`version-bump-deferred` (FLAG/decision, carried from Phase 1).**
  `_version.py`/`CITATION.cff` still NOT bumped; v0.3.0 is not releasable until
  Phase 3 lands. api-stability-reviewer flagged the bump "before merge" — that
  is the release step, intentionally deferred.
- **`D1d-minimal-threading` (decision).** v0.3.0 threads a *static*
  field through `TickScheduler`; NO `Field`/`AbstractSession`/seam objects. The
  whole interaction-engine architecture (doc 05 §1–§2) stays forward (~v1.0).
  The static array is the seam's zero-source degenerate case -> no rework.
- **`test4-structural-scope` (decision).** Test #4 deliberately scoped to the
  structural check (see flag above), with exact eigenvalues at exp_03.
